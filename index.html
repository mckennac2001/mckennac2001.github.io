What's this Frenemy stuff?

Frememy is a way for you to create outdoor games and play against your friends. You play by getting to certin location points defined in the game, before your frenemies. You can run, cycle, climb, sail, whatever... to these points. Basically you can create a game that uses any transport you can think of.

Anyone can create a game and invite their frenemies to compete against them. But remember, never create a game you don't intend to win
